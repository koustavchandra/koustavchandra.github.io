Ciao! I'm your friendly neighborhood gravitational-wave astrophysicist, diving deep into the cosmos to unravel the secrets of _gravity_, _black holes_, and _neutron stars_. By day, I’m crafting ways to detect and exploit [LIGO](https://ligo.caltech.edu/) and [Virgo](https://www.virgo-gw.eu) measurements to answer questions in (astro)physics and fundamental physics, while paving the way for future instruments like [CE](https://cosmicexplorer.org/) and [ET](https://www.et-gw.eu/). By night... well, you guessed it right, I binge-watch TV Series as if it is an Olympic Sport.
